COMP 426 Minesweeper

Instructions:

  • Set the size of the board and number of bombs (min required: 8x8 max: 30x40)
  • Click "Start Game" to begin
  • Click on a random button to see if it has a bomb or not.
  • If it doesn't, it will say how many bombs are adjacent to that square.
  • Your goal is to find out where each bomb is in the board without clicking on it.
  • Shift + click marks a square to remind yourself there is a bomb there. Shift + click again to unmark it.
  • The game ends when you either click on a bomb (lose) or uncover all spaces without a bomb and mark every space with a flag (win!)
